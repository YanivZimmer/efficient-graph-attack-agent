| 8 | 2026-06-29 | inner-loop | Fixed the clustering gate to support probability-only candidate selection and added validation-threshold tuning in the experiment runner. | 9 | 2026-06-29 | inner-loop | Implemented five HGAT-family research variants and ran a unified AIT-ADS sweep. Best new result was `temporal_causal_hgat` at cluster F1 `0.4089`, followed by `multiview_hgat` at `0.4051`. Both beat the local calibrated baseline; `temporal_causal_hgat` also slightly beat the repo’s saved HGAT baseline. None approached GraphWeaver or the supervised GRAIN-style upper bound. |
| 10 | 2026-06-29 | inner-loop | Implemented and tested two v2 follow-ups. `multiview_hgat_v2` improved slightly over the saved HGAT baseline at cluster F1 `0.4070`, but still trailed `temporal_causal_hgat` v1. `temporal_causal_hgat_v2` improved node metrics but regressed to cluster F1 `0.4040`, suggesting the added temporal continuity/order losses are oversmoothing rather than improving incident separation. |
| 11 | 2026-06-29 | inner-loop | Implemented inference-time cluster ablations for giant-cluster splitting and relation-graph components. Best fair/non-semantic result was `multiview_hgat_v2 + dbscan_split_time_6h` with cluster F1 `0.8842`, up from `0.4070` before splitting and roughly matching the saved supervised GRAIN-style upper bound. Semantic/tactic ablations reached `0.9791` but are marked diagnostic because AIT tactic/technique can be derived from attack-step labels in the current loader. |
| 12 | 2026-06-29 | autonomous-sprint | Alternated method improvement and new-approach tests. Improvement: added `4h` temporal episode strategies and confirmed `multiview_hgat_v2 + temporal_only_4h` / `dbscan_split_time_4h` at cluster F1 `0.9363`, precision `0.9517`, recall `0.9392`, beating the prior fair best and saved GRAIN-style reference on AIT-ADS. New approach: Bayesian-block event segmentation was a strong negative result, over-fragmenting into roughly `1000+` micro-clusters with best multiview F1 `0.0195`. |
| 13 | 2026-06-29 | inner-loop | Implemented a weak-label-only `macro_elbow` temporal gap estimator that ignores telemetry micro-burst gaps and detects prominent macro-gap jumps in the selected alert stream. On AIT-ADS, `multiview_hgat_v2 + temporal_only_macro_elbow` exactly matched the fixed `4h` result at cluster F1 `0.9363`; saved HGAT improved slightly to `0.882013`; temporal-causal HGAT improved over fixed short gaps but still trailed its `48h` setting. |
| 14 | 2026-06-29 | inner-loop | Ran two further iterations. Iteration 1 added chronological 4-fold holdout validation and showed unconstrained macro-elbow is not robust: multiview mean F1 `0.7692` versus fixed `4h` `0.9515`. Iteration 2 added `macro_elbow_floor4`, which prevents small-gap over-fragmentation and achieved the best multiview holdout mean F1 `0.9588` while preserving the full-slice F1 `0.9363`. |
